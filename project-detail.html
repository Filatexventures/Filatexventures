Door-to-door moving services

After you have booked your move with us and arranged for your household goods to be picked up, you can sit back, relax, and let us handle the rest.

What we’ll do for you: Pick up and transportation to/from port: Be it by rail or truck, we’ll cover the journey of your household goods to and from the ports of origin and destination.

Customs paperwork:

We’ll prepare and submit all customs-related documents for your household goods in both the country you’re moving from and country you’ll soon be calling home.

Handle the ocean freight journey:

We’ll ensure the container in which your household goods are transported is guaranteed a spot on the shipping vessel and liaise on your behalf with the shipping carrier.
